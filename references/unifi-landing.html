How It Works

Simple enough for
a 2 AM emergency.

Three roles. One platform. Money moves in under 30 minutes.

1
🎓
Verify Your Campus Identity
Sign up with your university email. Upload your student ID. Our admin reviews and approves you — usually within a few hours. No Aadhaar, no CIBIL, no nonsense.
For Borrowers & Providers
2
💸
Request or Browse Loans
Borrowers set an amount, pick a tenure (7, 14, or 30 days), and see the exact repayment upfront. Providers browse anonymous loan requests and fund the ones they like.
Borrowers request · Providers browse
3
⚡
Money Moves via UPI
Provider sends to UniFi escrow. We release to the borrower instantly. On due date, borrower repays through UPI. Provider receives principal + returns. UniFi takes a small cut.
Secure · Traceable · Fast

Built for Trust

We take your money
seriously.

Every layer of UniFi is built to protect both sides of every loan.

🎓
Campus-Only Network
